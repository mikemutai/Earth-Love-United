About ELU

Pioneering sustainable solutions for a regenerating planet

Earth Love United is a global regenerative sustainability foundation dedicated to healing our planet through science-driven initiatives, verified carbon offsetting, and community empowerment. We unite researchers, communities, and organizations in the fight against climate change.

Scientific Rigor

Evidence-based research and transparent methodologies ensure every initiative delivers measurable impact.

Global Reach

Operating across multiple continents with partnerships in 4 major carbon registries worldwide.

Regenerative Focus

Moving beyond sustainability to actively restore ecosystems and regenerate environmental health.

Our Mission & Vision

We exist to accelerate the transition from extractive practices to regenerative systems. Through innovative research, strategic partnerships, and community engagement, we create scalable solutions that restore planetary health while empowering local communities.

Our Vision

A world where human activity actively heals the planet—where every carbon credit, every research project, and every community initiative contributes to a thriving, regenerative future for all.

Research Initiatives

Seven core areas of environmental innovation driving real-world impact

Mangrove Restoration

Coastal ecosystem regeneration projects sequestering carbon while restoring marine habitats and protecting coastlines.

Algae Biofuels

Advanced algae cultivation systems converting CO2 into sustainable biofuels and high-value bioproducts.

Methane Capture

Innovative biogas recovery systems transforming agricultural and industrial waste into clean energy sources.

Biofertilizer Development

Natural soil enrichment solutions reducing dependency on synthetic fertilizers while improving crop yields.

Photovoltaic Innovation

Next-generation solar technology research maximizing energy capture efficiency and reducing installation costs.

Thalassa Initiative

Ocean health monitoring and restoration programs protecting marine biodiversity and blue carbon ecosystems.
